tulum,before it was tulum

Amansala was founded in 2004 by Melissa Perlman on a stretch of beachfront that most people hadn't heard of yet. No algorithm. No influencer campaign. Just a vision — that people needed a place to move their bodies, clear their minds, and actually rest.

Twenty years later, we're still here. Same beach. Same belief that transformation doesn't require a spa menu that reads like a pharma catalog.
