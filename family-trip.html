amansala family trip

summer camp

for the whole family

Leave the routines behind. At Amansala, families rediscover each other through shared adventure, unhurried mornings, and the kind of togetherness that only the Caribbean coast can inspire.

things to do

adventures for
every age

From crystal-clear cenotes to ancient ruins, every day brings a new way to play together.

🌊

cenote swimming

Plunge into hidden underground pools surrounded by limestone and jungle canopy. A natural wonder the whole family will remember forever.

🚣

kayaking the coast

Paddle together along the turquoise Caribbean coastline, gliding past mangroves and spotting sea turtles in the shallows.

🏛

mayan ruins tour

Walk among the ancient clifftop temples of Tulum. History comes alive when you can reach out and touch the stones.

🍳

beach cooking class

Learn to make traditional Yucatecan dishes together, from fresh ceviche to handmade tortillas, feet in the sand.

🚴

jungle bike ride

Cycle through lush jungle trails to hidden cenotes and local villages. An adventure that feels like a secret.

🪨

snorkeling

Explore the vibrant reef just offshore. Tropical fish, gentle currents, and warm water make it perfect for all skill levels.

explore

wander together

Step beyond the resort and into a world of wonder. Tulum is a place where ancient jungle meets the Caribbean Sea, and every path leads to something extraordinary.

Whether you are bargaining for handmade treasures at a local market or standing atop a cliff where the Maya once watched the horizon, these are the moments your family will talk about for years.

  • 🏜 Tulum town — art galleries, taquerias & local life
  • 🌳 Jungle trails — cenotes, wildlife & hidden paths
  • 🌊 Caribbean coastline — white sand stretching for miles
  • 🛒 Local markets — handcrafted textiles & spices
  • 🏛 Mayan ruins — clifftop temples above the sea

relax

slow down,
breathe deep

Family vacations should leave you rested, not exhausted. At Amansala, there is always space to simply be — together or in peaceful solitude.

Mornings begin with yoga on the sand as the sun rises. Afternoons dissolve into hammock naps and ocean swims. And evenings? Those belong to golden sunsets watched from the shore, toes in the warm sand.

  • 🕶 Spa treatments — massages, facials & body wraps
  • 🧗 Beach yoga — sunrise sessions for all levels
  • 🏖 Beach lounging — sun, shade & ocean breeze
  • 🛌 Hammock naps — swaying between the palms
  • 🌅 Sunset watching — the Caribbean sky in full colour
