about amansala

Tulum's first
beachfront resort

What started as a 4-room pop-up has become one of the most iconic beachfront resorts in Tulum. Renowned for its wabi-sabi design using organic materials, we provide guests with an authentic, rejuvenating experience.

Whether you join one of our yoga and wellness retreats or simply book a room, you'll find community and a place you want to revisit again and again. Amansala is a perfect blend of authenticity and simplicity meets comfort and style.

our story

A few duffel bags
and a dream

In 2001, Melissa Perlman arrived with a few duffel bags of what she calls "pretty things" to set up a few caravanas on the beach in the sleepy town of Tulum.

She followed her instinct and a deep passion for creating a holiday with a purpose — one that sends you home looking and feeling better than when you arrived.

More than 20 years later, that vision still guides everything we do.
