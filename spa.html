At Amansala Spa, we bring together the ancient wisdom of Mayan healing traditions with modern spa therapies to create one of the most unique wellness experiences in Tulum.

Located directly on the beach, our open-air spa allows guests to relax to the sound of the ocean while receiving deeply restorative treatments. Every experience is rooted in intention, nature, and healing.

Whether it's a solo self-care escape, a romantic retreat, or a bachelorette getaway — our treatments restore balance, release tension, and leave you feeling renewed
